About AI-BUDDY

Your Journey to Mindful Wellness

AI-BUDDY is a revolutionary digital wellness platform that combines artificial intelligence with proven psychological approaches to help you develop a healthier relationship with food and emotions.

Comprehensive Features:

  • Personalized AI Coaching: Receive tailored guidance based on your unique patterns and needs
  • Mood & Food Tracking: Understanding the connection between emotions and eating habits
  • Extended Fasting Programs: Guided fasting experiences with daily support
  • CBT & Mindfulness Tools: Evidence-based techniques for emotional regulation
  • Progress Visualization: Track your journey with intuitive charts and insights

Our Approach:

We combine cutting-edge AI technology with established psychological principles to provide:

  • Real-time emotional support and guidance
  • Personalized strategies for managing emotional eating
  • Comprehensive wellness tracking and insights
  • Community support and shared experiences
