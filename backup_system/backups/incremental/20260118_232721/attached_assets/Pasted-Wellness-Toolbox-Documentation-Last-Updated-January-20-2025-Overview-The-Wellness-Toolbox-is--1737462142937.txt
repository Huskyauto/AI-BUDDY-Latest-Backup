Wellness Toolbox Documentation
Last Updated: January 20, 2025

Overview
The Wellness Toolbox is an integrated feature of AI-BUDDY that provides comprehensive stress monitoring and meditation tracking capabilities. It supports both smart ring integration and manual stress logging to accommodate all users.

Core Features
1. Stress Monitoring
Smart Ring Integration
Real-time stress level monitoring via Oura and Ultrahuman rings
Automatic stress level tracking during meditation sessions
Current stress level display (0-100 scale)
Average stress reduction metrics
Manual Stress Logging
Simple 1-10 scale stress level input
Symptom tracking with common options:
Headache
Muscle Tension
Fatigue
Anxiety
Optional notes field for detailed context
History view of last 6 stress entries
Temporary success/error notifications (2-second display)
2. Meditation Features
Guided Vipassana meditation sessions
Customizable session durations:
10 minutes
20 minutes
30 minutes
45 minutes
60 minutes
Background sound options:
Gentle Rainfall
Wind in Trees
Flowing Water
Brown Noise
Ambient Pad
Volume control for background sounds
Automatic stress level measurement before and after sessions (if ring available)
Manual stress logging integration for non-ring users
3. Progress Tracking
Meditation statistics:
Current streak
Longest streak
Total sessions
Total minutes
Average stress reduction (if applicable)
Stress history display:
Timestamp
Stress level
Associated symptoms
Notes
Most recent 6 entries shown
4. Integration with Community Features
Link to meditation challenges
Community participation tracking
Shared progress metrics
